About us

Selsey book club is a collection of reading groups and consists of a number of people living in the neighbourhood who read and talk about books based on a topic or an agreed-upon reading list. The groups choose a specific set of books to read from the library and discuss at the same time. They meet monthly in order to give members time to read the next book. Some of the groups are focused on literary critique and some on less academic topics. If you love reading, you can always join an existing group or start your own group or just be a member and borrow books.

Features

Well-Being

Human engagement is essential for our mental well-being. The club bring together the book lovers in the neighbourhood. Some of these members have organised themselves into groups and they meet quite regularly to discuss the books they are reading.

Coffee!

Books and coffee complement each other. So when you come to pick up new books or to return the old ones, get some coffee and give yourself some time to explore the different titles we have. We have £1.00 coffee for book lovers!

Library

The book club maintains a good library and you can borrow books from it. For a small monthly membership fee of £5.00, you can enjoy unlimited access to the library. We also accept second hand books as donations.

Listen on Audible New!

For those who find it hard to read, we now offer stories on audible. We take requests from members and can schedule hearing sessions for you. Please contact us to drop in a request.
