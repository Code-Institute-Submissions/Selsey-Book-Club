About us

Selsey book club is a collection of reading groups and consists of a number of people living in the neighbourhood who read and talk about books based on a topic or an agreed-upon reading list. The groups chooses a specific set of books to read from the library and discuss at the same time. These groups meet monthly in order to give members time to read the next book. Some of the groups are focused on literary critique and some on less academic topics. Most of the groups are focused on fiction. One of the groups is dedicated to a particular author. Whatever reading material you prefer, if you can't find a book club for it why not think about starting your group within the book club ?

Features

Well-Being

Human engagement is essential for our mental well-being. We connect our members to each other via facebook groups and now we have a few groups that are connected on facebook and meet quite regularly at different outdoor venues to discuss the books they are reading.

Coffee...coming soon!New!

Books and coffee complement each other. So when you come to pick up new books or to return the old ones, get some coffee and give yourself some time to explore the different titles we have. We are soon introducing £1.00 coffee for book lovers. Watch this space!

Library

The book club maintains a good library and you can borrow books from it.For a small membership fee of £5.00, you can enjoy access to the library. We also accept second hand books as donations.

Listen on Audible

For those who find it hard to read, we also offer stories on audible for specific groups at specific times. The announcement for such events can be found on the Library page.
